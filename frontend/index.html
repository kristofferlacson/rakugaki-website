Our Story

Inspired by the Japanese word "rakugaki" — meaning doodle — Rakugaki is a celebration of playful imagination expressed through culinary mastery. Each dish is designed as a work of art, merging refined Japanese tradition with modern creativity.

Founded in 2010 by Master Chef Hiroshi Yamamoto, our restaurant has been awarded three Michelin stars for seven consecutive years, recognizing our commitment to excellence and innovation in Japanese cuisine.

Dining with us is not simply eating — it is experiencing the harmony of flavors, textures, and aesthetics that tell a story with every bite.

Hiroshi Yamamoto, Executive Chef
